Performed as a sunflower in the school play. Forgot two lines but covered it with an enthusiastic dance!

Wearing that tiny cap and gown, feeling so grown up. Little did I know how much I'd miss those days.

Our visit to the science museum. The planetarium show sparked my interest in astronomy.

My first inter-school debate. Nervous start but we placed second arguing about school uniforms.

Math Olympiad

Representing our school at regionals. The challenging problems pushed me to think in new ways.

Middle School Graduation

High School Start

Everything felt bigger and more serious. Walking in with middle school friends made it easier.
